Benefits

#1 Enhanced Aesthetic Appeal:

Exterior house rendering instantly enhances the aesthetic appeal of your home. Whether you prefer a sleek contemporary finish or a timeless classic look, rendering provides a versatile solution to suit your style. With a wide range of colors and textures available, you can customize the appearance of your home to reflect your personality and preferences.

#2 Increased Property Value

Investing in exterior house rendering is not just about improving the look of your home; it’s also a smart financial decision. A beautifully rendered exterior can significantly increase the value of your property, making it more attractive to potential buyers should you ever decide to sell. It’s a simple yet effective way to maximize your home’s resale value.

#3 Protection from the Elements

Rendered exteriors offer more than just visual appeal; they also provide durable protection against the elements. From harsh sunlight to heavy rain, rendering acts as a protective barrier, safeguarding your home from weather damage and helping to maintain its structural integrity for years to come. Say goodbye to cracked paint and weather-worn facades rendering provides long-lasting protection for your home’s exterior.

#4 Improved Energy Efficiency

Did you know that exterior house rendering can also help improve the energy efficiency of your home? By adding an extra layer of insulation, rendering helps to regulate indoor temperatures, keeping your home cooler in summer and warmer in winter. This not only enhances comfort but also reduces energy consumption and lowers utility bills, saving you money in the long run.

#5 Low Maintenance

Tired of constantly repainting your home’s exterior? With exterior house rendering, maintenance becomes a breeze. Rendered surfaces are easy to clean and require minimal upkeep, allowing you to spend less time on maintenance chores and more time enjoying your beautifully transformed home.

From initial consultation to final installation, we are committed to providing an unparalleled level of service and satisfaction.

Types of Rendering

Rough Casting Render

Rough casting render, also known as pebbledash, uses a mixture of sand, cement and stones to create a textured finish to your exterior walls. The benefits of this render include durability and weatherproofing as well as creating a visually appealing rustic charm. 

Brick Effect Rendering

Adopt brick effect rendering to your exteriors for a timeless and classic finish. Our experts will create the appearance of traditional brickwork by applying a render mix and using a specialist technique to create grooves or patterns to resemble the mortar joints between bricks. This method is far more cost-effective than actual bricklaying but with the same aesthetic appeal.

Brick Slips

Get the look of traditional brick walls, without the weight or expense of full-sized bricks. Brick slips can be applied to various surfaces, both interior and exterior, providing versatility while maintaining the timeless charm of an authentic brick wall.

External Insulation and Rendering

Improve the energy efficiency of your home as well as its curb appeal with external insulation and rendering. Our dedicated team will apply insulation boards to the exterior walls, followed by a layer of render to boost weatherproofing and aesthetics. This method helps reduce heat loss, which could lower your energy bills!
