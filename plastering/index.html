Types of Plastering

Drylining and Plasterboarding

Smooth and neat walls, guaranteed! Plasterboarding offers an efficient solution for creating a sturdy foundation for your walls. We’ve been plasterboarding for over 30 years, so you can be confident you’ll be getting a professional and smooth finish, ready for you to decorate.

Skimming

The final touch before painting over, skimming adds a flawless finish to your walls. Walls and ceilings that have suffered wear and tear can also be reskimmed to give a nice, neat finish, making your wall look new again.

Float Finished Backing 

This method provides a solid foundation for further plastering or decorative finishes, ensuring a uniform surface. Float finished backing allows for precise control over the thickness and texture of the plaster, resulting in a durable and visually appealing interior space.
